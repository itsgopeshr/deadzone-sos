Get Your Offline Map

Search for your city or region below. We will redirect you to a secure map that you can save directly to your phone before the internet goes down.

Why do this?

During a flood or earthquake, streets may be blocked. Having a local map saved on your device ensures you can navigate to safety even with zero cell tower signal.

How to Save the Map

  • Step 1
    Type your city name in the box and tap "Search & Download".
  • Step 2
    The Google Maps app or website will open. Look at the top right corner and tap your Profile Picture or the Menu (⋮).
  • Step 3
    Tap "Offline maps", then tap "Select your own map" and hit Download. The map is now safe on your phone forever!
