⚠️ Mandatory Setup (Do This Now)

To ensure this app works when cell towers fail, you must initialize it and save it to your phone right now while you are online.

🤖 Android / Chrome

1. Tap the 3-dot menu (⋮) at the top right of your browser.

2. Tap "Install app" or "Add to Home screen".

🍎 iPhone / Safari

1. Tap the Share icon at the bottom of the screen (square with an arrow pointing up).

2. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen".

✅ Final Step: Open the app from your home screen once to initialize the offline engine!
